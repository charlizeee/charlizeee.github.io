FAVORITE CMSC SUBJECT

With the number of CMSC subjects that I have taken in the past years,
CMSC 123 has become the subject I have come to love the most. As
a batch 2020 student, I was not able to start my Computer Science
career in a face-to-face setting. The unfortunate circumstances at that
time forced all students, including myself, to learn behind computer
screens. That kind of set-up is not by any means ideal for a technical
course like computer science. As our circumstances as a society got
better, I finally had the chance to take a CMSC course face-to-face. It
has honestly been very difficult. But, to my surprise, I was able to learn
more and appreciate the art of my course in a better sense.
